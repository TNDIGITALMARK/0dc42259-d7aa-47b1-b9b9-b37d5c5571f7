- Cloud hosting (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure)

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### User Acquisition
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)**: Target <$20
- **Free â†’ Pro Conversion Rate**: Target 5-10%
- **Pro â†’ Premium Conversion Rate**: Target 2-5%

### Retention
- **Monthly Churn Rate**: Target <5%
- **Average Customer Lifetime**: Target >18 months
- **Lifetime Value (LTV)**: $180+ for Pro, $360+ for Premium

### Revenue Health
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Track growth month-over-month
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**: Predictable income
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Ratio**: LTV/CAC should be >3

## Pricing Optimization Strategies

### 1. Annual Discounts
Offer 2 months free for annual plans:
- Pro: $99.99/year (save $19.89) - 17% discount
- Premium: $199.99/year (save $39.89) - 17% discount

**Benefits**: Improves cash flow, increases retention, reduces churn

### 2. Student Discounts
- Pro: $6.99/month (30% off)
- Premium: $13.99/month (30% off)

**Benefits**: Builds brand loyalty early, expands market

### 3. Family Plans
- Pro Family (up to 5 users): $19.99/month
- Premium Family (up to 5 users): $34.99/month

**Benefits**: Higher overall revenue, increased stickiness

### 4. Corporate Wellness
- Enterprise pricing for companies
- $7.99 per employee/month (minimum 50 employees)
- Bulk analytics and admin dashboard

**Benefits**: Large, stable revenue source

## Risk Mitigation

### Churn Management
- **Grace periods**: Don't immediately lock users out after failed payment
- **Win-back campaigns**: Offer discounts to churned users
- **Exit surveys**: Understand why users leave

### Competitive Threats
- **Stay innovative**: Continuous feature development
- **Build community**: Network effects reduce switching
- **Premium content**: Exclusive, high-quality content is hard to replicate

### Market Changes
- **Diversify**: Don't rely on one customer segment
- **Monitor trends**: Stay ahead of fitness and wellness trends
- **Pivot readiness**: Be prepared to adjust pricing and features

---

## Conclusion

Your subscription revenue should be reinvested strategically across infrastructure, product development, support, and marketing to build a sustainable, growing business. The key is balancing short-term profitability with long-term growth investments.

**Key Takeaways**:
1. **Infrastructure (17.5%)**: Foundation for reliability
2. **Product Development (27.5%)**: Continuous improvement
3. **Marketing (22.5%)**: Fuel growth engine
4. **Support (12.5%)**: Keep users happy
5. **Reserve (10%)**: Build financial resilience

**Next Steps**:
1. Track all expenses by category
2. Calculate actual margins per tier
3. Monitor LTV/CAC ratios monthly
4. Adjust pricing based on market feedback
5. Reinvest profits into highest-leverage areas
